Written Response

3a.

3.a.i.

This program allows users to added reviews on recipes they found on our site. They can rate the recipe out of 10 and add a short comment summarizing what they felt after creating the recipe.

3.a.ii.

In the video, I showed the ability to search for a recipe and add a review for said recipe. The program takes a rating out of 10, a short comment, and their name. The program then uses a POST Request to add the input to a database. It then uses a GET Request to retrieve that data and formats it into a table.

3.a.iii.

The inputs of the program are the rating, the comment, and the user's name.
